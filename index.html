Impact of Social Media on People

Social media has definitely changed the world in the last decade. In this narrative visualization we will look at the inception of social media networks and how social media has impacted the way people spend their time.

The narrative structure being used is an interactive slideshow, which is a hybrid structure in the narrative visualization spectrum.

You can navigate through the slides either by using the "Next" and "Previous" buttons at the top or by using left and right arrow keys on your keyboard

This presentation has been tested to work on both Chrome and Firefox browser with a screen width of 1100px or higher.

Created by Sahan Serasinghe

Inception of Major Social Media Networks

We will first look at how social media networks have grown from its inception. With a total global population of 7.7 billion there are at least 3.3 billion people online. It is reported that Facebook alone has reached 2.6 billion monthly active users in 2020.

Social media networks such as MySpace who dominated the space has disappeared by 2008 and competitors such as Facebook has remained dominant in the last decade. It is also worth noting that new social networks such as TikTok has reached 500M in 2018 within a year.

The below chart depicts the growth of the prominent social media networks. You can drill down on a particular year using the slider as well as see their growth from 2004 to 2018 as an animation using the Play button.

Who Uses Social Media

Now we will look at trends in social media consumption by different age groups in the US. Overall, we can see a growth in the recent years in social media consumption by different age groups. It is worth noting that young adults of ages 18-29 range were the early adoptors of social media platforms we looked at in the previous slide. They also continue to be the biggest propotion of users

Usage of older adults have also grown due to the increased familarity with social media platforms.

A Different Perspective

In this chart we look at the distribution of the usage of different social media platforms among Men and Women in the US. Even though dominant players such as Facebook and Youtube are popular amoung Men and Women, Facebook remains the most popular option among Women and Youtube among Men.

Other competitors such as Pinterest and Reddit also has the same effect and we can see that different social media networks have had an impact on gender wise.
